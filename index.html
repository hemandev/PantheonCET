ABOUT

PANTHEON 3.0

ICI CET students' chapter in association with ICI Trivandrum chapter presents to you the Techno extravaganza, PANTHEON 3.0, an inter college competition exclusively for the civil engineering students all over India on September 30 to October 1st 2016. The competition seeks talent exhibiting potential to be titled the best among the amateur civil engineers who are at the doorsteps of entering the professional world. Here is the chance to showcase your resourcefulness, techniques and spontaneity and win attractive cash prizes. So come conquer and make your mark.


ICI CET STUDENT's CHAPTER

The Indian Concrete Institute chapter was opened in College of Engineering Trivandrum in 2013, for the nurturing of budding civil engineers.In 2015 ICI CET Chapter won the BEST EMERGING CHAPTER award and In 2014 It has acted as a platform where ideas are shared, theories are questioned and classroom knowledge is challenged. Talks by experts in the civil engineering field are frequently arranged, which have proved to give the students not only technical knowledge but also valuable insights into the professional world that help them to choose their career paths. The industrial visits that are arranged in and outside the city have turned the textbooks and pictures into reality for the students. Competitions always sharpen the edges and elevate talents and that is what we have achieved through the competitions that we have arranged within the chapter. Now we are ready to take it up to the next level and widen the horizon through our new venture, Pantheon.

event x



On 30th Sept
Are you an inspiring Civil engineer? Do you have what it takes to make it as an entrepreneur? Then this is the place to be! In this event, not just your civil engineering knowledge, but also your entrepreneurial skills will be put to test. Leadership skills, time management, resource allocation and general knowledge will play a major role in deciding the winners. The first round will be a written round based on basic civil engineering questions, followed by elimination. In the second round, the teams will have to complete a series of tasks to achieve an end goal, thus deciding the team skills and resource management capabilities. In the third round, the teams will have to come up with innovative ideal solutions to a Civil Engineering problem given to them. The fourth and final round will be a start-up based quiz. The team with maximum points from the last three rounds will be adjudged as winners.
